About Me

Hello There! My name is Kshiti Patel, a third year science student at the University of Waterloo. I am looking to work in nano-application R&D. I have experince with nanoparticle & nanowire synthesis as well as thin film depositions. Currently I am searching a Summer coop and full-time position after May 2022. My passion, however, is photogrpahy. I started photography with my first camera at the age of 11. I always enjoyed being behind the camera and taking in the magical moments life offers. A decade later, I have created an Instagram page (@kshitiipatelphotography) where show my work. My aim for this website is to challenge myself to capture moments with a story behind them to share. I also aim to display my research in photographic blogs. Please feel free to contact me with an inqueries on kshiti@hotmail.ca or DM on instagram to connect!
